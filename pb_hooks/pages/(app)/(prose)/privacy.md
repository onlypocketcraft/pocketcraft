# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 27, 2024**

At PocketCraft, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

## 1. Introduction

PocketCraft provides educational content and tutorials for PocketBase development. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage

### 2.1 Information We Collect

- **GitHub Information**: When you become a sponsor, we receive basic information from your GitHub profile.
- **Usage Data**: We collect anonymous analytics data to improve our content and services.

### 2.2 How We Use Your Information

- **Content Access**: We use GitHub sponsorship status to manage access to premium content.
- **Communication**: We may send updates about new content and features through GitHub.
- **No Third-Party Sales**: We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third parties.

### 2.3 Content Analytics

- **Usage Statistics**: We collect anonymous data about which content is most viewed and engaged with.
- **Improvement**: This data helps us create better content and improve the learning experience.

## 3. Platform Security

### 3.1 Hosting Environment

Our platform is hosted on PocketHost and uses PocketBase for data storage.

### 3.2 Data Protection

- **Content Security**: All premium content is securely stored and access-controlled.
- **HTTPS**: All connections to our platform are encrypted using SSL/TLS.
- **Authentication**: Access to premium content is verified through GitHub's OAuth system.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking

- **Essential Cookies**: We use cookies only for authentication and essential platform functionality.
- **Analytics**: We use basic analytics to understand content usage patterns.
- **No Ad Tracking**: We do not use any advertising trackers.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

- **No Selling of Personal Data**: We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.
- **Third-Party Service Providers**: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 6. User Rights

- **Access and Correction**: You have the right to access and correct your personal data at any time.
- **Data Portability**: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Deletion**: You may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal obligations.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date.
